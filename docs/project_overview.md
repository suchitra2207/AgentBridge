# Project Overview

## Problem Statement

Workplace challenges such as stress, burnout, communication barriers, discrimination, and career uncertainty affect millions of employees worldwide.

Organizations collect feedback through surveys and HR systems, but converting this information into meaningful action remains difficult.

## Solution

AgentBridge is a multi-agent AI platform that helps employees receive personalized workplace guidance while enabling organizations to identify recurring concerns through anonymized insights.

## Benefits

### Employees
- Faster access to workplace support
- Personalized recommendations
- Increased awareness of available resources

### Organizations
- Early detection of workplace issues
- Data-driven decision making
- Improved employee engagement
